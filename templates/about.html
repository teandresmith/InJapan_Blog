About Me

My Story?

Well, you are probably wondering what my story is... The truth is I am just an ordinary person that grew to love Japan because of martial arts, anime, and manga. Yeah, it's that simple, but I'll dive into details about a few things.

When did I move to Japan?

When I decided to join the Air Force, I finally got the opportunity to visit Japan in June 2018. I was super shocked when I got notice about moving to Okinawa, Japan, but if I told you that I was only excited, I would definitely by understating my emotions at the time. I was estatic about being able to visit and actually live in a place I dreamed of visiting! I still live in Okinawa, Japan today, but I have visited and experienced many parts of Japan since my arrival!

What is this Blog about?

This blog gives a real perspective about random topics here in Japan. Before moving to Japan, I could only imagine Japan being this super kind, and super peaceful country, but as I got to experience the culture, I began to realize that Japan has its issues just as any other country does. I want to give honest opinions about social issues, and many other topics that could possibly help others understand the dynamics of culture in Japan.

What do I do here?

As I mentioned before, I am in the Air Force, and actually work as a Jet Engine Mechanic, but I am also a college student in his senior year.

How long have I been studying Japanese?

Even though, I have been watching Anime for over a decade, I actually never attempted to learn Japanese until I arrived here in Japan. I started to learn the basic writing system like Hiragana and Katakana back in June-July 2018, but I didn't really take my studies seriously until mid 2019. I would say it has been about 1 1/2 years of seriously putting forth the effort day in and day out. I wouldn't say my Japanese is good, but it is definitely enough to get by and have fun here in Japan. I only plan on bettering my language abilities.

What's Next?

I actually am getting out of the military soon. I will also be graduating college with my bachelors in Computer Science pretty soon. I really want to get a programming job out here in Japan. My end goal is to enter game development as that has always been an aspiration of mine.
