Japanese Web Resources

WaniKani
Kanji | SRS

Japanese is comprised of three writing systems. Kanji, which derived from the Chinese Languages, and Hiragana/Katakana which are Japan's very own unique characters. For individuals that are interested in reading Japanese materials like Manga or looking to survive Japan in general. WaniKani is a great starting point as the websites focuses on helping users learn the most commonly used kanji in Japan through SRS learning. I can say for sure that WaniKani has helped developed my ability to recognize Kanji and it has also impressed many of the locals I have encountered.

Anki
SRS Flashcard

Anki is japanese for memorization, and this application specializes in just that. Another SRS flashcard system that focuses on consistent studying. For that specific reason, Anki is perfect for creating custom deck flashcards or to search into user shared decks from others.The application will allow individuals to quickly learn vocabulary, which you will need to know alot of, while being able to digest the language piece by piece.

Japanese Pod 101
Listening

Japanese Pod 101 is a subscription based japanese resource. There is many resources out there for listening, but I have enjoyed the collection of different series JPod 101 has. The best part about JPod101 is that it has content for every single level of japanese. From Beginner to Advanced, there is content that can be useful in bettering your listening abilities. At the same time, JPod101 has a mobile app and all of their content is accessible from it. If you are looking to improve your japanese, I think it is a decent resource.

Bunpro
Grammar | SRS

Bunpro is another SRS similar system that focuses specifically on grammar. Bunpro is actually a play off the japanese word "bunpou" which means grammar. As you can tell, I am a big fan of SRS systems because I believe that they are valuable assets when learning a language. Of course, one cannot learn a language with only SRS, but it definitely allows you to study a lot of information in a short amount of time efficiently!
