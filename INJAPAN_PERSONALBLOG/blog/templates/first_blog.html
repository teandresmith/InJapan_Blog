InJapan: What is it about?

Well, to say it simply, this blog is about supplying people around the world knowledge about Japan. This isn't the only blog about Japan, but the one thing this blog will be able to do is tell the truth, and only the truth. Before I came to Japan, I thought I had learned all about its culture through its sources of media...in other words, manga, anime, video games, movies and basic news sources such as NHK. But the truth is, those sources only displayed how great Japan was, and not the issues that are prevalent here. Most people who have never been to Japan or have visited for a short period of time, see Japan as this super peaceful and nice country. I won't deny that the people of Japan are nice, but I feel the passive-aggressive mindset of Asia in general goes unnoticed by foreigners. This isn't me trying to bash Japan or anything, but this is an attempt to shine the light on real life experiences as a foreigner who lives and sees Japan on a daily basis. With that said, my focus is to provide real, raw-uncut information about Japan's culture and society. At the same time, I will like to provide bite-size information on the Japanese language and other random topics pertaining to my experiences here in Japan.
